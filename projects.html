_Ragesquared

"A game named after its base components. Your objective is to click on a white square, and no other shapes, while on a timer that steadily grows faster as decoy shapes start appearing."

This was a fun project. I made this game in LOVE2D with just a little help from a friend to get started (the same friend also composed the music) and i think the end result is great. Massively inspired by Super hexagon and quite functional (and finished too! Not very many projects get that privilege). Definitely one of my prouder accomplishments.

_Laborinth

"An assymetric 3d heist game. A thief is on a mission to steal a dangerous virus from a lab guarded by a cheese starved sequrity crew with remote access to the security system. "

In the spring of 2018 i participated in the Global Game Jam on the a which made Laborinth, which I would by no means call a masterpiece, there was a lot of polish missing in the end, but I had a very enjoyable time nonetheless. In this project I was in charge of 3D modelling and level design.
